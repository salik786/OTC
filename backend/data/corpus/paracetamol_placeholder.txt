Uses
Paracetamol 500mg Tablets are used for the temporary relief of mild to moderate pain,
including headache, toothache, period pain, and pain associated with colds and flu.
It is also used to reduce fever (high temperature).

Dosage
Adults and children 16 years and over: take 1 to 2 tablets every 4 to 6 hours as needed.

Frequency
Do not take more often than every 4 hours. Do not take more than 4 doses in any 24-hour period.

Max Dose
Do not take more than 8 tablets (4 grams) in 24 hours. Taking more than the recommended dose
can cause serious liver damage.

Warnings
Do not take with any other paracetamol-containing products. Do not exceed the stated dose.
If symptoms persist for more than 3 days, or you have a high fever, consult a doctor or
pharmacist. Keep out of reach of children. If you take too much, contact a doctor or hospital
straight away, even if you feel well, because of the risk of delayed, serious liver damage.

Storage
Store below 25 degrees C, in a dry place, away from direct sunlight. Keep the blister pack in
the outer carton until required.

Missed Dose
If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember, then continue with your normal dosing
schedule. Do not take a double dose to make up for a missed one.

Side Effects
Side effects are rare when taken at the recommended dose. Stop use and seek medical advice if
you notice any allergic reaction such as rash, swelling, or difficulty breathing.
