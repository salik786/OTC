Uses
This Daily Multivitamin is a dietary supplement used to help support general health and
supplement the diet with essential vitamins and minerals, including Vitamins A, C, D, E, and
the B-complex vitamins.

Dosage
Adults: take 1 tablet daily.

Frequency
Take once per day, preferably with food.

Max Dose
Do not take more than 1 tablet in 24 hours. Taking more than the recommended amount does not
increase benefit and may cause side effects.

Warnings
Do not take if you are allergic to any of the ingredients listed on the pack. This product is
not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Keep out of reach of children.
If you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or taking other medication, check with a pharmacist or
doctor before use.

Storage
Store in a cool, dry place below 25 degrees C, away from direct sunlight. Keep the container
tightly closed.

Missed Dose
If you miss a day, simply resume your normal daily dose the next day. Do not double up.

Side Effects
May cause mild stomach upset in some people if taken on an empty stomach. Stop use and consult
a pharmacist if you experience any unusual symptoms.
